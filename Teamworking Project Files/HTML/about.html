About

The Must Eat takeaway is a fast food takeaway that prides itself on excellent service and delicious food. We have restaurants in both the Perth city centre and Stanley.

We serve delicious food, from burgers to curries, pizzas to chicken. With our online store you can order from the comfort of your own home and we'll bring the food right to you, or you can choose to collect. We have great ratings and have patrons from all over Perth, so you can be sure that you will be happy with your order!
